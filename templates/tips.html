Cyber Security Tips

🔒 Always Use HTTPS

Only enter passwords and personal information on websites that use HTTPS.

📧 Beware of Phishing Emails

Never click unknown links received through email or SMS.

🔑 Use Strong Passwords

Create passwords with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

🔄 Keep Software Updated

Install the latest security updates for your operating system and browser.

🛡 Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Use 2FA whenever available to protect your online accounts.

🌐 Verify Website URLs

Check the domain carefully before entering login credentials or payment details.
